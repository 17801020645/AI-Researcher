innovative ideas:
We propose the Rotation Trick for VQ-VAEs: instead of the straight-through estimator (STE), propagate gradients through the vector quantization layer by smoothly transforming each encoder output into its nearest codebook vector via a rotation and rescaling linear transformation (treated as constant during backprop). This preserves the angle between the gradient and codebook vector, improving reconstruction quality, codebook utilization, and reducing quantization error across multiple VQ-VAE training paradigms.
